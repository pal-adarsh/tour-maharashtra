Popular Activities

Trekking

Maharashtra is home to stunning trekking destinations like Rajmachi, Lohagad, and Harishchandragad. Explore the Sahyadri ranges and enjoy breathtaking views and historical forts

Food

Maharashtra's diverse cuisine ranges from spicy street food like vada pav to traditional delights like puran poli. Experience the flavors that define the state’s culinary identity

Ganesh chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi is Maharashtra’s most vibrant festival, celebrated with grand processions and artistic idols. Witness the culture and devotion during this 10-day festivity

Water Parks

Imagica

Imagica is a world-class theme park offering thrilling rides, live shows, and immersive experiences. It’s perfect for families and adventure seekers alike, with attractions ranging from roller coasters to themed restaurants.

Wet n Joy

Wet n Joy, located in Lonavala and Shirdi, is a massive water park featuring high-speed water slides, wave pools, and relaxing lazy rivers. It’s the ideal spot for a fun-filled day in the sun.

Water Kingdom

Water Kingdom in Mumbai is Asia’s largest water park, with an array of exciting water rides, a giant wave pool, and attractions for all ages. It promises endless entertainment and a refreshing escape
